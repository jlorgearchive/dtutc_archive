Who is Jamie Lorge?

The website and the YouTube channel is part of a series that dives into the question: "What if Five Nights at Freddy's was real?". I've been a fan since the release of the first game, and like some of the younger fans at that point, I always wanted to believe that the place was actually real. Welp, now that I'm more competent, why not make it real myself?

I am not from the United States so the documents I produce is likely inaccurate. If you've spotted any mistakes (lore-wise, document-wise), or if you have brilliant ideas about what to add to Universe 87's archive or this website, feel free to send me an email via jlorgearchives@gmail.com.
