About the website

The website and the YouTube channel is part of a series that dives into the question: "What if Five Nights at Freddy's was real?". I've been a fan since the release of the first game, and like some of the younger fans at that point, I have always wanted to believe that the place was actually real. Well, now that I'm more competent, why not make it real myself?

If you have spotted any mistakes (lore-wise, document-wise), you have brilliant ideas about what to add to Universe 87's archive or this website, you are a copyright holder who I forgot to credit/wishes for the removal of your property on this website, or you just want to contact me, feel free to send me an email via jlorgearchives@gmail.com. Thank you!

Unless specified above, everything displayed on the website and the YouTube channel was painstakingly created and recreated by me. If you wish to share anything, be sure to link back to my website or the YouTube channel, and if you can, email me about it so I can like it :D
