Project Universe 87

Description

The project imagines an alternate universe where the events of "Five Nights at Freddy's" actually happened and the video games are based on real events. The project aims to recreate videos, photos, documents, Internet and other media in that alternate universe.

Between The Closure and The Games (1993 - 2014)
Following the closure, the company continued very limited operations. In the years since then and before 2014, the company, its restaurants and its events became an urban legend, making it a mystery to many, especially those outside of Utah. This mysterious aspect combined with nostalgia gave the company a small following on the Internet. During this period, the company also sued people who try to investigate its past

The Scott Cawthon games (2014 - 2022)
Date Event
August 8, 2014 This alternate universe's Scott Cawthon developed "Five Nights at Freddy's", a horror "point-and-click" game based on the urban legends of the company. The first game is set during the restaurant's final year of operation in 1992
November 11, 2014 Scott Cawthon then released a sequel called Five Nights at Freddy's 2. The second game is set in 1987, right before the "Bite of '87"
June 19, 2015 Scott Cawthon then released the third game called Five Nights at Freddy's 3. This universe's FNAF 3 is the equivalent of our universe's FNAF 4, meaning the game is set in Evan Afton's bedroom in 1983, immediately before "The Bite of '83". Five Nights at Freddy's 4 or our universe's FNAF 3 was never released in this universe.
October 7, 2016 Five Nights at Freddy's: Sister Location was released and is set at Circus Baby's Entertainment and Rental in the early 1990s
The video games, especially the first installment, became popular with many YouTubers like Markiplier, PewDiePie, MatPat making videos about the game. The games also generate an active fan community for the video game series and the company itself. Fazbear Entertainment chose to not do anything about the games. The video games's lore were based on speculations by Scott himself so while they are similar to our universe's lore, not every detail is correct. This universe's Scott Cawthon also released book series about the games.

"Event Knowledge" symbol meaning:

I: Information only to those interested in the restaurants and its company's history (hard-core fans, researchers, etc.)
L: Information only known to locals (mostly Hurricane residents but can also be where the restaurants operated)
U: Information completely unknown by anyone outside of certain people (indicated in table) or none at all
