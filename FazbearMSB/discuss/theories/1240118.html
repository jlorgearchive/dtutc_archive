I'm not here to weep about how tragic the events were because I'm sure we all know at this point. SO let's dive into a deeper topic. Who. Was. The killer?

Well, I'm sure we have a few suspects. We know it HAD to have been an employee since the beginning considering they knew precisely A: how the yellow suits worked, and B: where they even were, but we know it has to have been a FORMER employee since Stanley Hall stated that all employees were busy, though he also lied about the springlock suits not being used so...that's unreliable. But, hey, narrows it down.

Now, if what CrazyManiac heard was true, there was an accident back at the Fredbear's Family Diner location, where one of Afton's son lost their life because of his brother. Why is this relevant, you may ask. Well, according to this news clipping that I found (Spectrum's May 12th, 1985), around the same time the 85MCI happened, two teenagers went missing suspiciously near the La Verkin Creek Trail. While it's most likely these incidents were just coincidentally correlated, I have a feeling these incidents are more linked. I believe that William Afton, one of the owners, has something to do with these missing children incidents. That's just a hunch though, though it IS worth noting the killer does know about the springlock suits considering he used one, and I'm pretty sure William had an attachment to the yellow bonnie, AND the fact that CrazyManiac mentioned he lived "near the forest of La Verkin". What do you lot think?
