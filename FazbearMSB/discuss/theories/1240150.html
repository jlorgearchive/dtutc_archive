Hi all, it's my first post here after lurking for a while collecting info for my own research... sooooo let's go straight to the theory. William Afton, the man who started it all, the one who brought us here, is dead. Sad. But how do I know this? By looking at the facts and applying common sense.

For crying out loud, the man has been AWOL for ten years! He can legally be presumed dead! CrazyManiac, Goldiebear21, and many more have lived in Hurricane and its surrounding area for years and yet there are literally zero, absolutely no signs of him on the news or from the other locals, nada.

People don't just fall off the face of the Earth like that without a good reason. I don't buy the whole "laying low" or "moving to another state" some people have here. Afton is in his 50s, he's got plenty of time! He's been an entrepreneur and innovator all his life. He loved his robots. He literally founded another animatronic entertainment business after leaving one. He wouldn't just quit his passion like that.

Here's where I'll venture to the more "speculative" area: How he died. Could it be another animatronic accident like his kid in 83? Would've been poetic in a way: dying from your creation...

Or... BIG OR... he died investigating the 92MCI. Maybe he got a bit too close to the truth. A man like him would probably try to find out the cause of the bad things that happened to his former business which gave him fame.
