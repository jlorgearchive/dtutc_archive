Sure thing!

It was really an "all or nothing" move by them. They've got billboards everywhere, ads in the papers, and even commercials on TV. They really went all out on the whole transparency theme in the campaigns. They held a demonstration of the new facial recognition feature for the press, even started a program where they bring kids backstage and explain to them how the animatronics work to entertain and keep them safe.

They were also pretty serious about improving security measures, too. Each location got a big security office right at the end of the hallway of the "Party Zone." I remember taking pictures of the security guards and their massive camera systems.

Unfortunately, they probably wasted every last bit of their budget for the campaign which is why the restaurants got so bad before mass closure.
