on November 25th, 2002, 1:27am, Mikey_A wrote:
I've heard about William Afton having some sort of sister restaurant after he left the company.

You're thinking of Circus Baby's Pizza World, a restaurant that Mr. Afton wanted to open after he sold the restaurant but was unsuccessful because of a gas leak that happened a few days before opening day. animdude's "William Afton after Freddy's" topic discusses more in detail and has some pretty interesting speculations if you're interested.

There were definitely other animatronic restaurants in other states that had plans to open but strangely never did. A friend of mine from San Jose told me about a restaurant called Chucky's Treehouse that had big plans in '84 and that they were going to be "California's Freddy Fazbear". Then, a few weeks before opening day, everything stopped. Same thing happened to some restaurant called Showtime Palace in Missouri.

I'm not saying it was definitely Freddy's, but...
