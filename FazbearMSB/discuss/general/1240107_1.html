During the last few months of 1982, they did have a version of The Puppet that would greet any customers at the front door, especially during large parties. The "Security Puppet" I think it was called. I can also recall it giving out bracelets of different colors (like those elastic bands you would get at concerts). You will have to let it scan your bracelets to be let in, otherwise, staff will be called. The run didn't last long, though. After Charlie's disappearance, they stopped using it after finding it laying outside in an alley nearby, completely broken. Nobody's sure why it was outside in the first place.
